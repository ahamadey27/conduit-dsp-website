FAQ

What platforms are supported?

Our plugins support Windows (VST3, AAX) and macOS (VST3, AU, AAX).

What DAWs are compatible?

Any DAW that supports VST3, AU, or AAX formats — including Ableton Live, Logic Pro, Pro Tools, FL Studio, Reaper, Studio One, and more.

How do I install a plugin?

After purchasing, download the installer from your account. Run the installer and follow the on-screen instructions. Restart your DAW and the plugin will appear in your plugin list.

What is the difference between Robin Control and Robin Control Lite?

Robin Control Lite is a free version with core round robin management features. Robin Control includes the full feature set with advanced randomization control and additional options.
