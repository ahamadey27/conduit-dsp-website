# **Privacy Policy**

Last updated: April 2026  ·  This policy applies to conduitdsp.com and all Conduit DSP products.

Conduit DSP LLC ("Conduit DSP," "we," "our," or "us") is a small, independent audio software company based in New York. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it. We keep things simple because we are a small operation and we only collect what we actually need.

If you have questions about anything here, email us at hello@conduitdsp.com.

## **1\. Information We Collect**

**Email address.** When you download a free plugin or join a waitlist, we ask for your email address. This is how we send you your download link and occasional updates about our products. That is the only personal information we collect directly.

**Purchase information.** If you buy a paid plugin, your payment is processed by Lemon Squeezy (our payment provider). We receive confirmation that a sale occurred and the email address associated with the order. We do not store or see your credit card number, billing address, or any other payment details — Lemon Squeezy handles all of that.

**Usage data.** Our website may collect standard web analytics data — pages visited, time on site, referral source, browser type, and general geographic region (country/region level only). This is aggregated and not tied to your identity. We use this to understand how people find us and which content is useful.

**Support communications.** If you email us for support, we keep that email thread so we can follow up and improve our products. We do not share those conversations with anyone.

## **2\. How We Use Your Information**

* To send you your plugin download link after you sign up  
* To send product updates, version releases, and occasional announcements (you can unsubscribe at any time)  
* To process and fulfill paid orders via Lemon Squeezy  
* To respond to support requests  
* To understand how our website is being used so we can improve it

We do not sell your email address or any personal information to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising targeting on other platforms.

## **3\. Third-Party Services We Use**

We use a small number of third-party tools to run our business. Each has its own privacy policy that governs how they handle data:

* **MailerLite** — Email marketing and list management. Your email address is stored in MailerLite when you subscribe. [MailerLite Privacy Policy](https://www.mailerlite.com/legal/privacy-policy)  
* **Lemon Squeezy** — Payment processing and license key delivery for paid products. [Lemon Squeezy Privacy Policy](https://www.lemonsqueezy.com/privacy)  
* **Namecheap** — Domain registration and website hosting infrastructure.

We are selective about the tools we use. We do not use Facebook Pixel, Google Ads tracking, or any behavioral advertising scripts on this website.

## **4\. Cookies**

This website may use minimal cookies for basic functionality (such as remembering your cart state or language preference). We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies. If you use a browser that blocks cookies, the site will still work.

## **5\. Data Retention**

We keep your email address on our list for as long as you remain subscribed. If you unsubscribe, your address is removed from our active list. Purchase records are retained as required for business and tax purposes (typically 7 years, in line with U.S. tax law).

## **6\. Your Rights**

You can unsubscribe from our email list at any time using the link at the bottom of any email we send. You can also email us to request that we delete your data, correct inaccurate information, or find out exactly what information we hold about you. We will respond within 30 days.

If you are located in the European Union or United Kingdom, you have additional rights under GDPR, including the right to data portability and the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. Email us and we will work with you directly.

## **7\. Children**

Our products and website are not directed at children under 13\. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 13\. If you believe a child has submitted information to us, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

## **8\. Changes to This Policy**

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top and, if the changes are significant, notify our email list. Continued use of our site or products after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
